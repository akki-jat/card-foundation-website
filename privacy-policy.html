Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Contact us through our website forms
  • Make a donation to our foundation
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Apply for our training programs
  • Volunteer with our organization

2. Types of Information

The types of information we may collect include:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, postal address
  • Financial Information: Payment information for donations (processed securely)
  • Program Information: Information related to training program applications
  • Communication Records: Records of communications with our organization

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide our services and respond to your inquiries
  • Process donations and send tax-deductible receipts
  • Communicate with you about our programs and impact
  • Send newsletters and updates (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except:

  • To trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website and programs
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication procedures
  • Staff training on data protection practices

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Unsubscribe from our communications
  • Request information about how we use your data

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.
