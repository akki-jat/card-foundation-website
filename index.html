About CARD Foundation

Creating lasting change through skill development, environmental conservation, and community empowerment

Our Story

CREATIVE ACTION FOR RURAL DEVELOPMENT FOUNDATION (CARD) was established with a vision to bridge the gap between opportunity and accessibility. We believe that every individual, regardless of their background, deserves the chance to develop skills, contribute to environmental conservation, and be part of a sustainable future.

Through our comprehensive approach combining skill development with environmental stewardship, we've created a unique model that not only empowers individuals but also protects our planet's precious resources and wildlife.

Our Mission

To empower individuals through skill development, promote environmental sustainability, protect wildlife, and conserve native species of birds. We create opportunities for youth in both rural and urban areas, helping them acquire skills for a brighter future while fostering environmental consciousness.

Our Vision

A world where skilled communities live in harmony with nature, where every youth has access to opportunities, and where environmental conservation is a shared responsibility.

Our Key Programs

Comprehensive initiatives designed to empower communities and protect our environment

Skill Development Programs

Empowering youth and adults with market-relevant skills through certified training programs that open doors to better employment opportunities and entrepreneurship.

  • PMKVY 4.0 Training Programs
  • HARTRON Skill Development Center
  • Digital literacy and technology training
  • Entrepreneurship development workshops
  • Vocational training in various trades

Environmental Conservation

Leading tree plantation initiatives and environmental awareness campaigns to combat climate change and create a sustainable future for our communities.

  • Large-scale tree plantation drives
  • Native species restoration projects
  • Environmental awareness campaigns
  • Sustainable farming practices training
  • Climate change mitigation programs

Wildlife & Bird Conservation

Dedicated to protecting native species of birds and wildlife through conservation programs, habitat restoration, and community education initiatives.

  • Native bird species conservation
  • Wildlife habitat restoration
  • Animal welfare programs
  • Biodiversity protection initiatives
  • Wildlife rescue and rehabilitation

Youth Engagement & Awareness

Conducting comprehensive training and awareness camps for youth in rural and urban areas to build environmental consciousness and social responsibility.

  • Youth leadership development programs
  • Environmental awareness camps
  • Community mobilization initiatives
  • Social entrepreneurship training
  • Volunteer engagement programs
